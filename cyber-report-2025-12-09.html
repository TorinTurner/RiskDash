Risk Calculation Reference

HIGH Risk

  • Breakfix > 0 - Any asset in either enclave in breakfix
  • No VRAM Scan Data - Unable to assess risk without scan data
  • TAM Past Due > 20 - Optional HIGH trigger (adjustable)

MEDIUM Risk (2 or more of the following)

  • VPH > 3.5 - Vulnerabilities per host above threshold
  • Product/Policy Compliance < 95% - ESS compliance below threshold
  • Scan Age > 14 days - VRAM scan older than threshold
  • TAM Past Due > 10 - Too many overdue TAMs

LOW Risk

  • VPH < 2.5 - Low vulnerabilities per host
  • Scans within 14 days - Recent scan data
  • Compliance > {low_ess_compliance}% - High product and policy compliance
  • No HIGH risk triggers present

Key Calculations

  • Scan Age = Days since last VRAM scan date
  • VPH = Vulnerabilities Per Host (from VRAM RA VPH column)
  • True Policy % = PolicyEnforced Ã— ProductCompliance / 100

Scan Exempt

If a boat is marked Scan Exempt, HIGH risk is downgraded to MED only when HIGH is due to "No VRAM Scan Data". Breakfix or other HIGH triggers (like TAMs > 20) are NOT affected by Scan Exempt status. For NMCI boats, both NIPR and SIPR must be exempt to apply the override.
