If a boat is marked Scan Exempt, HIGH risk is downgraded to MED only when HIGH is due to "No VRAM Scan Data". Breakfix or other HIGH triggers are NOT affected by Scan Exempt status. For NMCI boats, both NIPR and SIPR must be exempt to apply the override.
